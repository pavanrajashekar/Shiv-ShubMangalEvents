The Atelier

Eleven years. A thousand stories.

From an intimate mehendi in a quiet courtyard to a five-thousand-guest gala under the stars — every milestone has shaped how we craft yours.

2014

A small idea. A big heart.

Founded in a one-room studio with a single laptop, a leather notebook, and one wedding to plan.

2016

A sunset in Udaipur.

Three days. Seven ceremonies. A baraat on elephant-back and a bride who still writes us, every year.

2019

Two hundred and fifty stories.

A growing family of designers, florists, chefs, choreographers and quiet, patient storytellers.

2022

Beyond celebrations.

Tech launches, leadership offsites and brand spectacles produced with the same devotion.

2025

Today. And tomorrow.

1,200+ celebrations across 14 cities. One belief: every moment deserves to be remembered.
